Rentgeniux Onboarding Automation — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What data we access

This extension accesses your Gmail inbox to read contact form submission emails sent to your property management email address.

What we do with your data

Email content is sent to Groq AI API and your configured backend server only for classifying property leads into Owner, Tenant, or General categories. Emails are labeled and marked as read in Gmail.

What we do NOT do

We do not sell your data. We do not store emails on any third-party server. We do not send emails on your behalf. We do not delete any emails.
